Spin Precession in magnetized Kerr spacetime

Time: 2025-05-02

Authors: Karthik Iyer, Chandrachur Chakraborty

Abstract: This study presents an exact analytical investigation of spin precession for a test gyroscope in magnetized Kerr spacetime, revealing that magnetic fields modify precession frequencies by interacting with gravitoelectromagnetic structures, induce distinct behaviors near black holes versus naked singularities (potentially aiding their observational differentiation), introduce long-range \(1/r\) corrections to gravitomagnetic effects, and enhance geodetic precession in Schwarzschild spacetime (\(\propto r^{1/2}\)), offering testable predictions for black holes in strong magnetic environments like those near magnetars or in the early universe.

Innermost stable circular orbit of arbitrary-mass compact binaries at fourth post-Newtonian order

Time: 2025-05-02

Authors: Luc Blanchet, David Langlois, Etienne Ligout

Abstract: This study employs standard Taylor-expanded post-Newtonian (PN) methods without resummation to compute the innermost stable circular orbit (ISCO) of arbitrary-mass compact binaries at 4PN order, demonstrating results consistent with gravitational self-force (small mass-ratio) and numerical-relativity (equal-mass) calculations, while also detailing the harmonic-to-ADM coordinate gauge transformation, including 4PN tail contributions.
